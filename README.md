# PrimeTrade.ai Hiring Assignment

## Project Title

Trader Behaviour Analysis Based on Bitcoin Market Sentiment

---

## Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze historical trading data together with the Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index to understand how market sentiment influences trader behaviour and trading performance.

---

## Datasets Used

### 1. Historical Trader Data
Contains trading information such as:
- Account
- Coin
- Execution Price
- Size (USD)
- Side (Buy/Sell)
- Closed PnL
- Timestamp

### 2. Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index
Contains:
- Date
- Fear & Greed Value
- Market Sentiment Classification

---

## Project Workflow

1. Imported required libraries.
2. Loaded both datasets.
3. Explored dataset structure.
4. Cleaned and formatted the data.
5. Converted timestamps into date format.
6. Merged trader data with market sentiment data.
7. Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA).
8. Created visualizations.
9. Generated business insights and conclusions.

---

## Visualizations

The following visualizations were created:

- Market Sentiment Distribution
- Buy vs Sell Analysis
- Top 10 Traded Coins

- Average Trade Size by Sentiment
- Top Profitable Coins
- Buy vs Sell Profit Comparison
- Correlation Heatmap
- Top 10 Traders by Profit

---

## Key Insights

- Market sentiment influences trader behaviour.
- Buy trades occur more frequently than sell trades.
- A few cryptocurrencies dominate trading activity.
- Trade size changes across different sentiment conditions.
- Profitability differs under Fear and Greed market conditions.
- Some traders consistently outperform others.

---

## Conclusion

The analysis indicates that Bitcoin market sentiment has a noticeable relationship with trading behaviour and profitability. Understanding Fear and Greed conditions can help traders improve decision-making and manage trading risk more effectively.
